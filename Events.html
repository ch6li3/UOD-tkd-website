EVENTS

The grading system is used to show your advancement in Taekwondo. We hold local school gradings 3 times a year which are conducted by Miss Tate and Mr Dolan. These are held locally at the end of October and Mid-March, and at our summer camp the 1st weekend in June. There are a further 2 regional gradings conducted by a Master, Senior Master or Grand Master. These are held the 1st Saturday in December and May.

Twice a year, following the regional grading we hold a seminar conducted by a Master, Senior Master or Grand Master. This is your opportunity to train with our associations senior practitioners. These are held the 1st Saturday in December and May.

We host local competitions as an introduction to competing twice a year – normally mid November and March. The idea is for everyone to have fun and enjoy seeing how they fair against their peers. For those that wish to compete more widely we attend national and international competitions across Scotland and Ireland.

F.A.T Training stands for ‘Fitness and Taekwondo’ training. This session takes place on pre-arranged Saturday afternoons at Ward Road Gym. Attire is Dobok (training suit) bottoms and t-shirts. Bring plenty of water, a towel and plenty of energy. Be prepared to sweat more than normal…

The first weekend in June (Monday-Friday) we host a residential training camp in Moffat. This camp is open to Taekwon-do students who wish to spend a weekend training and learning. It is a fully packed weekend that includes stretching, fundamental movements, breaking, patterns, sparring and Taekwon-do has talent. If you are prepared to join in, adhere to the tenets of Taekwon-do and learn a lot this weekend is for you.

Fight Night and Christmas Party.
Taking place in early December our Fight Night and Christmas Party involves team sparring and self defence demonstrations, followed by a buffet, disco and loads of fun!

Halloween and Welcome Party.
Time to dress up in your weirdest and most wonderful Halloween costume! The evening involves Taekwon-do games and cake!

Annual Instructors BBQ/Gathering.
A chance to talk Taekwon-do, chill-out and mix in the Scottish sunshine!

Posts local competition gatherings.
Food and refreshments in our sponsors establishement and a chance to review your competitive performance.
