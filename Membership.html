MEMBERSHIP

Price

We offer a trial period in September where you can join us and see if Taekwon-do would suit you.


The society's membership fee is £45.

*To train with us you also need membership for Institute of Sport and Exercise. The subscriptions available are "Sport" or "Sport & Exercise". Please consult ISE for more information.

Approach

Taekwondo is reputed to be one of the most effective forms of self-defence and our Instructors are passionate about teaching what works. They believe that you can apply the martial art to the sport but you cannot apply the sport to the martial art.

Environment

The Instructors work hard to create and maintain a Taekwondo family. As part of this family unit you will be encouraged to push yourself and learn in a safe environment to move out of your comfort zone and achieve things you didn’t know you could do.

Results

Taekwondo is a great balancer. This means you will get out of it what you put in. The more you put in the more you will gain. People are often surprised at the benefits they receive. There are the obvious physical benefits of increased fitness and flexibility but 95% of martial art training is mental…
