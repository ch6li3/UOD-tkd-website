FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. Do I have to be physically fit before joining the class?

No, that is not necessary; your fitness will improve through the training and as long as you attend regularly.

2. Do I have to be physically flexible?

No - your flexibility will improve with training.

3. Do I need to have any special kind of uniform?

Initially, you may wear something that is loose-fitting such as a track bottoms and T-shirt but should you decide to continue training, you will be required to wear the Taekwondo uniform which we call ‘Dobok’ plus a belt which will be white for beginners. These can be purchased from the instructors.

4. Where are the classes held?

The classes are conducted in the ISE building every Tuesday and Thursday from 8.00-9.00pm.

5. I’ve heard there are different styles of Taekwon-do, which style do you train in?

We follow the teachings of the Founder of Taekwon-do – General Choi Hong Hee – as laid out in the 15 Volume Encyclopedia that he wrote. Our club is lucky to have an excellent Taekwon-do lineage. Our Instructors train with First Grand Master Rhee Ki Ha, the Father of European Taekwon-do and the first person to be promoted to Grand Master by the Founder, and Grand Master Paul Cutler, who is the first person to be promoted to Grand Master by First Grand Master Rhee.

6. Am I too old to start?

There is no age barrier starting to train in Taekwon-do as long as you wish to learn and make the commitment to attend class regularly, you will learn a lot and have a lot of fun.

If you have any additional question don't hesitate to contact us!
