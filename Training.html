TRAINING SCHEDULE

Our philosophy: Open door…

We welcome anybody that wishes to learn the martial art of Taekwon-do. To try a class please get in touch with the Instructors and they can advise you which one would be best for you to attend.

What’s expected

1st lesson: everyone will welcome and encourage you. Wear loose sports clothing and bring some water. We train in bare feet and we recommend that you tie/clip long hair back. Please remove all jewellery. (If you can’t remove it then please tape it.)

Conduct and frequency: everyone follows the tenets of Taekwondo: Courtesy, Integrity, Perseverance, Self-control and Indomitable spirit. For good progression in the martial art we recommend that you train twice a week in class and complete some at home practise. (20-30 minutes daily)

Absenteeism: if you are not going to be able to make your chosen class then please let the Instructors know either at your previous class, by phone, text or Facebook message

We welcome all those that wish to learn Taekwondo; both new and those returning to study the art.
